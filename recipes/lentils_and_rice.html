Lentils and Rice

Description

This lentil and rice dish is a simple meal using inexpensive ingredients. Filling and nutrious, this meal packs fibre, protein, carbs, and fats into one tasty dish!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Brown Lentils
  • 4 cups of rice
  • 1 Onion (either Sweet or Red)
  • 5 Garlic Cloves
  • 3 tbsp of Basil
  • 1 tbsp of Thyme
  • Tap Water (as necessary)

Steps

  1. Add lentils to large, 4 Litre pot
  2. Set the pot on the burner to boil on HIGH
  3. Add the herbs and spices to the pot and stir
  4. Once the lentils have reached a rolling boil, turn down the heat to LOW, and reach a simmer
