Lentils and Rice

Description

This lentil and rice dish is a simple meal using inexpensive ingredients. Filling and nutrious, this meal packs fibre, protein, carbs, and fats into one tasty dish!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of brown lentils
  • 4 cups of white rice
  • 1 onion, chopped (either Sweet or Red)
  • 5 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 3 tbsp of basil
  • 1 tbsp of thyme
  • 1 cup of kale, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • Olive oil (as necessary)
  • Tap Water (as necessary)

Steps

    Lentils:

  1. Add lentils to large, 4 Litre pot
  2. Fill the pot about 2 inches from the top
  3. Set the pot on the burner to boil on HIGH
  4. Add the herbs and spices to the pot and stir
  5. Once the lentils have reached a rolling boil, turn down the heat to LOW
  6. Let lentils simmer for 12-15 minutes (depends if you prefer harder or softer lentils)
  7. Drain with a strainer and store in containers

    Rice:

  1. Add rice to large, 4 litre pot
  2. Rinse and wash rice until water is reasonably clear
  3. Drain any leftover water from the washed rice
  4. Add 6 cups of water to the washed rice
  5. Set the pot on the burner to boil on HIGH
  6. When the pot starts to boil, turn the heat to LOW
  7. Let the rice simmer for 15 minutes
  8. Remove from heat
  9. Fluff rice with a fork

    Mix:

  1. Put a large saucepan on the stovetop at MEDIUM-HIGH heat
  2. Once warm, add olive oil (use however much you prefer)
  3. Add chopped kale, onions, mushroom, and garlice
  4. Add 2 cups of cooked lentils
  5. Add 1-2 cups of cooked rice
  6. Add 1 egg
  7. Stir
  8. Once egg has cooked, add a small amount of olive oil to prevent dryness
  9. Transfer mixture to a bowl or plate, still leaving the saucepan at MEDIUM-HIGH heat
  10. Add 1 egg to the empty saucepan
  11. Scramble egg and add whatever spices you prefer
  12. Once egg is cooked, add to the cooked rice and lentil mixture
  13. Enjoy?
